IKEv2 IPv4-in-IPv6 tunnel with compression (IPCOMP).

The tunnel endpoints are IPv6 while the protected subnets are IPv4.

The outbound outer ESP SA (transport mode, IPv6) was missing the
XFRM_STATE_AF_UNSPEC flag, which is required for cross-address-family IPsec.
Without it the kernel rejects the SA for IPv4 inner traffic, preventing
policy installation and tunnel bring-up.
